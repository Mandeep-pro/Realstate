💡 Feature Insights

🏠 Square Feet (40.35%)

The single most important factor. Each additional square foot adds approximately $350-400 to the property value. This is the primary driver in the Gradient Boosting model.

📍 Neighborhood (30.28%)

Location is critical. Properties in Downtown neighborhoods command 48% premium ($1.05M) vs Rural ($708K). This feature captures geographic and demographic influences.

💰 Price per Sq.Ft (19.59%)

An engineered feature that captures value density. High price-per-sqft indicates premium properties and premium neighborhoods with strong demand.

✨ Amenities Count (5.12%)

Additional amenities (pools, gyms, parking, etc.) add value. Each amenity adds approximately $20-30K to the property value in our model.

🛏️ Bedrooms (4.66%)

While important, bedrooms alone are less predictive than overall size. Value depends more on total square footage and location than bedroom count.
